partner_operator

Description

Table containing data about operators’ partner operators. Partner operators may have a record in the Operator table. If so, that record is controlled by someone who works for that partner operator. The information in this table is controlled by BC OBPS (Output-Based Pricing System) operators who should not have access to other operator’s records.
