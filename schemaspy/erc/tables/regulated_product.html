regulated_product

41 rows


Description

Table containing regulated and unregulated products. Regulated product means a product listed in column 2 of Table 2 of Schedule A.1 of the Greenhouse Gas Industrial Reporting and Control Act: https://www.bclaws.gov.bc.ca/civix/document/id/lc/statreg/249_2015. Unregulated products have been added to the dataset to assist in grouping some unregulated emissions for further analysis.
